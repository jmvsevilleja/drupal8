Credit Card Calculator

This calculator will help you quickly work out which credit card suits your lifestyle and finances. It also helps you estimate how much time and interest you can save if you repay more than your minimum repayment Conclusion: This calculator does not take into account credit card fees and interest free period. The minimum repayment is calculated based on the card balance and the minimum repayment % (percent) or $ (dollars) entered. It varies from month to month.
