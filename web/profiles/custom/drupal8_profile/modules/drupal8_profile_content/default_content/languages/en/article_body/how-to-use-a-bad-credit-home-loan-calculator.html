Having a bad credit is not a barrier to taking out a mortgage. There are lenders who are willing to look beyond your credit score and offer bad credit home loans. A bad credit home loan calculator can help you decide on which offers are best suited for your needs.

Bad Credit Home Loan Calculators - How Does it Work?

There are a number of mortgage calculators in the market and all of them work to provide useful calculations that you can use to compare home loan offers. Regardless of a bad credit, this home loan calculators give you an idea of your monthly repayment based on your inputs.

Home Loan Calculator Inputs:

A home loan calculator typically asks for three inputs to provide a simple computation of monthly repayments. These three inputs are as follows:
  • Loan amount. The amount you want to borrow
  • Interest rate. The annual interest rate divided by 12 to get the monthly interest rate
  • Terms. The life of the loan.

Other details may include the following:

  • Repayment frequency
  • Repayment type
  • Deposit amount
  • State where you want to make a purchase
  • Other fees such as ongoing fees and processing fees

Home Loan Calculator Results:

  • Monthly Repayment. Your calculated monthly repayment throughout the life of the loan.
  • Total Interest Payments. This is the total interest to be paid throughout the life of the loan.
  • Total Payments. This is the total interest and principal to be paid throughout the life of the loan.
The use of a home loan calculator is straightforward and really easy to understand. You only need to provide the loan amount, interest rate, terms, repayment type, and other details. Should you be a first time home buyer or simply doing an early research, think of rough numbers to fit your requirements and finances. The calculator can provide a flexible option as to weekly, fortnightly, or monthly repayments and different loan terms. This allows you to work with the calculator according to your specific financial situation.

Why you would a borrower with a bad credit use a home loan calculator?

Those with bad credit ratings are generally priced with higher rates of interest than those who have higher credit ratings. As a result, loans with higher loan amounts and longer repayment periods will cost more interest over the life of the loan. Thus, it is important for those with bad credit to use a home loan calculator to calculate exactly what they are about to pay on their loan based on interest rate and term of the loan for them to make smart decisions when choosing bad credit home loans.

Tip: If you have a bad credit, opt for a shorter repayment period so as to avoid paying too high total interest repayments.

Using a home loan calculator, borrowers with bad credit can find lenders that offer lower interest rates, know what their total interest payments are, and compare better loans that meet their needs. Also, the calculator can help borrowers find out if the loan is affordable. This can be done by entering your intended deposit and loan amount, you can easily see what rate and repayment amount is applicable to your condition. You can compare this amount to your monthly income and decide if you can afford the loan based on your monthly financial budget.
