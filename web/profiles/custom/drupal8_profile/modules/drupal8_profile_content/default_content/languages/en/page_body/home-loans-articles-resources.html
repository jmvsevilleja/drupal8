These are the main things you need to consider before applying for a home loan to buy a new house:

  • Research your options
  • Provide proof of income
  • Write a detailed budget
  • The lender will check your credit history - black marks on your credit rating could mean you will be charged highest interest rate.
  • The lender will check your savings history - do you have deposit?
  • The lender will check your employment information- are you in stable employment?
  • Calculate your net worth - make sure it is realistic
  • Make a sizeable deposit of at least 20% of the purchase price
